## Long (About page / media kit / podcast booking)

Billy & Maryruth Mitchell are a real married couple who coach real couples — specifically, the ones whose kids have grown up and moved out, leaving a quieter house and a marriage that might feel a little quieter too.

They know this season because they've lived it. When their youngest left home, they found themselves polite strangers who shared a mortgage — going through the motions, telling themselves they were "fine," and quietly missing each other. One night Billy finally said out loud what they'd both been afraid to admit: "I don't know who we are anymore without the kids." That honesty became the turning point.

What followed was real, sometimes awkward, often hilarious work — and it worked. Today, Billy & Maryruth genuinely have more fun, more connection, and a better marriage than they did in their 30s.

Now they help other empty-nest couples do the same through Empty Nest Marriage — their free guide (*Reignite: 5 Shifts for Empty Nesters*), their private community, live workshops, and their signature Reignite coaching program. Their approach skips the therapy couch and the corporate jargon in favor of real talk, a little humor, and a lot of heart — grounded in faith and built for couples who know their marriage isn't done growing.

As they like to say: this chapter — the one after the kids leave — can be the best one of your marriage. They mean that.
